Vegetarian Meatballs

No one ever knows these are vegetarian. These also make great cocktail meatballs in a slow cooker with sweet and sour, Asian, or barbecue sauce.

    Ingredients

  • 9 large eggs eggs
  • 5 cups bread crumbs
  • 3 cups finely chopped walnuts
  • 3 cups grated sharp Cheddar cheese
  • 3 medium onions, finely chopped
  • 2 cups cottage cheese
  • Â½ cup vegetable oil
  • 6 tablespoons minced garlic
  • 4 teaspoons dried basil
  • 2 teaspoons salt

    Steps

  1. Preheat the oven to 400 degrees F (200 degrees C). Lightly grease a baking sheet.
  2. Mix eggs, bread crumbs, walnuts, Cheddar cheese, onions, cottage cheese, oil, garlic, basil, and salt together in a bowl with your hands until thoroughly blended. The batter will be very stiff.
  3. Chill mixture in the refrigerator for 1 to 2 hours.
  4. Roll into 1-inch balls and place on the prepared baking sheet.
  5. Bake in the preheated oven for 8 minutes, turn meatballs, and continue to bake for another 8 minutes.
